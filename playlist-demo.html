How to Use

Creating a Playlist

1. Click the "Create Playlist" button

2. Enter a name (e.g., "My Favorites")

3. Optionally add a description

4. Click "Create"

5. Your playlist card will appear!

Adding Songs

1. Scroll down to the "Trending Artists" section

2. Hover over any song card

3. Click the "+" button that appears in the top-right corner

4. Select which playlist to add it to

5. You'll see a success notification!

Viewing & Playing
